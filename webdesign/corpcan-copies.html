Corporations Canada - Buy Copies

A revamp of the digital process of buying copies of coporate documents. Completed user testing, research, and results analysis of part of a summer co-operative education work placement in 2017 with Innovation, Science, and Economic Development Canada.

Tasks

  • Note taking for internal user testing — A/B testing of two options and then further testing of the full flow for confirmation of usability
  • Analysis of user testing results and reporting of these results
  • Accessibility testing using JAWS screen reader technology

Gallery

The main screen of Buy Copies, this page was A/B tested with icons/legend and with text in the availability column. Results were overwhelmingly positive for use of icons, convincing the client (CorpCan) of using icons despite initial reluctance.
