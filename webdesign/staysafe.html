Stay Safe COVID-19 Testing Initiative

A series of webpages for Norgen Biotek Corporation's COVID-19 testing services.

Requirements

  • Brand-consistent
  • Provide a pathway for indivividuals/families wanting testing and for businesses wanting to test their employees
  • Easily adjustable to add new locations or new variations on the service, such as predeparture travel testing

Features

  • A pre-screening questionnaire for the individuals or families, who are able to order directly from the website
  • No need to log in or make an account in order to check out as an individual
  • Email contact form for businesses for the offering of alternate pricing for bulk and/or repeat customers
