The Queen Bee is a new haptic audio experience in the form of a neckband, intended to enhance music and make it accessible to those in the Hearing Impaired community. It features a companion application for a dedicated music player and music visualizer to extend the experience of listening to music to up to three senses.

Features

  • Neckband device with multiple haptic vibration patterns
  • Comfortable fit for day-to-day use
  • Companion app with music player and visualizer for Android
  • Ability to sort music and create playlists within the app
