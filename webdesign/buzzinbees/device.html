Neckband Device

Our neckband is our primary product. It provides a physical experience for music through vibration feedback, generated from the bass frequencies of any song which is playing in the companion mobile application. It contains:


  • A bluetooth chip that allows the application to connect to the neckband
  • A haptic motor which dictates which vibration patterns are triggered, based on the data received by the bluetooth chip
  • Two haptic motors, running in parallel - one in each neckband arm
  • Gooseneck tubing in the neckband arms for a semi-custom fit

User Testing

Throughout our development process, we completed two user tests on the device and its components. The first user test was completed with the first life-size and weighted prototype. The second was completed to test the vibration strength and distinguishability of the vibration patterns. Lastly, we had planned a test for the full experience (device and application). We were unable to complete this test due to the COVID-19 pandemic, but the protocol was prepared. Test protocols and results are available below.
