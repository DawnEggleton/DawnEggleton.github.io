The Observation Trail

A short game made using A-Frame and Node.js for WebVR for a term-long project. Primary role was as the lead programmer and project manager. Ran from January 2019 - April 2019.

Requirements

  • Multi-device and multi-user
  • Used as a method for sharing knowledge (educational)
  • Intended for elementary grade students learning about biomes and habitats

Features

  • A guidebook, intended for desktop or mobile use, featuring Isotope.js filters (the 'Guide' role)
  • A desktop-only WebVR game with random generation of sounds, 'enemy' animals, scenary, and path for the 'Hiker' role to navigate
  • Multiple methods of 'defeating' the enemies, including consequences for incorrect actions
  • Proximity-based generation to stay within the confines of WebVR's loading capabilities
  • Time which leads to game end scenario if the players take too long
