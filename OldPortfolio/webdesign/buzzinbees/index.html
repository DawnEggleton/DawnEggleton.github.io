About the Queen Bee

The following video will talk you through the basics our project. It will introduce you to our team, our objectives, and our product. Afterwards, we will give you a demonstration of our project. The most important information - namely, our project objective and our project overview - has been provided in text format as well, for your convenience. Our product images, official documentation, and a product demonstration can be found further down the page.

Project Objective

  • To create a device that provides the user with vibration-based feedback dependent on the frequencies of the music being played in the Android application
    • Create an enchanced or different experience for traditionally auditory music
    • Allow for persons with different levels of hearing to experience music in a non-auditory way
    • Provide an audio visualizer in the application as a secondary non-auditory experience

Product Overview

  • A physical device that creates vibration based feedback from audio data
    • Device responds to signals send from a mobile application
  • A companion mobile application for Android that functions as a music player and includes an audio visualizer
    • Application reads auditory information and outputs decomposed signals to the visualizer and the physical device
  • The device is neckband-styled, with a focus on user comfort and minimal hindrances
